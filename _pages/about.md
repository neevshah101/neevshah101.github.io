Hi! I'm a 4th year undergraduate at the Indian Institut of Science Education and Research (IISER), Pune studying Physics and Astronomy. I am broadly intereted in gravitational wave astronomy and cosmology, especially the things we can learn about our universe through the new window of gravitational waves. Currently, I am working with Dr Susmita Adhikari on studying the prospects of core collapse in self interacting dark matter (SIDM) halos. This summer, I am very excited to start working on my MS thesis with Dr Ajith and the ICTS Astrophysical Relativity group on gravitational lensing of gravitational waves.

Outside of research, I love trekking and exploring new places. I also watch a lot of movies and tv shows.
