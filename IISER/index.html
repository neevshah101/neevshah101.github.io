research

I’ve done a few research projects during my undergrad. My first project was with Prof Sourav Chatterjee at TIFR, Mumbai on calculating the possibilities of detecting blackholes in binary systems with luminous stars through TESS due to the effects of self lensing, ellipsoidal variations and relativistic beaming.

I am currently working with Prof. Susmita Adhikari at IISER Pune on self interacting dark matter halos. I am using data from cosmological LCDM simulations to calculate the fraction of halos in isolated as well host enviroments that may core collapse by present day due to the effects of self-interactions.

I am also working with Prof. Jess McIver at UBC, Vancouver on creating a novel statistical distinguisher to accurately classify between different classes of glitches and GWs with the help of the bayesian parameter estimation algorithm and random forest

I will soon begin my 5th year Masters thesis project at ICTS, Benagluru supervised by Prof. Parameswaran Ajith in the Astrophysical Relativity group. I will be working on including the effects of lensing in inferring the population properties of compact binaries from GW observations. I am super excited to work on this topic!
